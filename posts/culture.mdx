# West African Music and Dance

## Rhythms That Inspire

Music is the heartbeat of West African communities. From drums to stringed instruments, each note tells a story.

## Traditional Dance

Dance accompanies every celebration, weaving tales of history, spirituality, and community together in movement.

## Festivals and Gatherings

Cultural festivals are the perfect place to witness authentic performances and connect with local traditions.
