### Best Places to Stay in Port Harcourt for Weekend Trips

#### 1. GRA Phase 2 & Phase 3  
**Best for Luxury, Comfort & Privacy**

GRA (Government Reserved Area) is one of the most prestigious neighborhoods in Port Harcourt.

**Why Stay in GRA**
- Extremely secure and peaceful  
- Premium serviced apartments  
- Close to top restaurants and lounges  
- Clean, tree-lined streets  

**Best For**
- Business professionals  
- Couples seeking upscale comfort  
- Event and corporate guests  

**What to Expect**
- Stylish, contemporary interiors  
- Smart TVs, fast Wi-Fi  
- Fully equipped kitchens  
- Secure gated compounds  

---

#### 2. Peter Odili Road  
**Best for Modern Luxury & Social Lifestyle**

Peter Odili Road is one of Port Harcourt’s fastest-growing premium areas.

**Why Stay Here**
- Modern residential estates  
- Secure, family-friendly environment  
- Close to waterfront and Trans-Amadi  
- Easy access to major parts of the city  

**Best For**
- Young professionals  
- Couples  
- Families  
- Lifestyle travelers  

**What to Expect**
- Hotel-style finishes  
- 24/7 power and strong security  
- Easy access to restaurants and lounges  

---

#### 3. Stadium Road  
**Best for Central Access & Convenience**

Stadium Road offers one of the most central locations in Port Harcourt.

**Why Stay Here**
- Easy navigation  
- Close to schools, clinics, lounges, and malls  
- Very safe environment  

**Best For**
- Business travelers  
- Families  
- Event visitors  
- Convenience-focused guests  

**What to Expect**
- Fully furnished apartments  
- Cozy interiors  
- Reliable power and water supply  

---

#### 4. Ada George  
**Best for Affordable Comfort**

Ada George is ideal for travelers seeking comfort without premium pricing.

**Why Stay Here**
- Budget-friendly shortlets  
- Quiet and relaxed environment  
- Easy access to Mile 4, Choba, and GRA  

**Best For**
- Budget travelers  
- Students  
- Groups attending weddings or events  

**What to Expect**
- Clean 1-bedroom apartments  
- Spacious rooms  
- Fully equipped kitchens  
- Secure compounds  

---

#### 5. New GRA / Abacha Road  
**Best for Social Life & Food Lovers**

New GRA is a hub for nightlife, dining, and social activities.

**Why Stay Here**
- Numerous lounges and restaurants  
- Lively nightlife  
- Safe and active environment  

**Best For**
- Young adults  
- Friends on weekend trips  
- Nightlife lovers  
- Social couples  

**What to Expect**
- Stylish studio and 1-bedroom apartments  
- Walkable access to entertainment  
- Serviced, comfortable units  

---

#### 6. Trans-Amadi  
**Best for Business & Oil & Gas Travelers**

Trans-Amadi is the commercial and industrial heart of Port Harcourt.

**Why Stay Here**
- Close to corporate offices  
- Secure and professional environment  
- Easy access to premium estates  

**Best For**
- Business executives  
- Oil & gas professionals  
- Corporate travelers  

**What to Expect**
- High-security serviced apartments  
- Business-friendly amenities  

---

#### 7. Rumuola / Rumuokuta / Mile 4  
**Best for Fast City Access**

These areas sit at the city’s movement core.

**Why Stay Here**
- Strong transport connections  
- Access to both leisure and business zones  
- Mixed affordability options  

**Best For**
- Travelers moving around the city  
- Event attendees  
- Practical, short stays
