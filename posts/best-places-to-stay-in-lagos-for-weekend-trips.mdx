### Best Places to Stay in Lagos for Weekend Trips

#### 1. Lekki Phase 1  
**Best for Beaches, Lifestyle & Trendy Urban Weekends**

Lekki Phase 1 is one of the most desirable neighborhoods in Lagos, blending entertainment, comfort, and modern living.

**Why Stay in Lekki**
- Close to popular beaches (Elegushi, Oniru)  
- Vibrant nightlife and restaurant scene  
- Secure estates and modern apartments  
- Quick access to Victoria Island and Ikoyi  

**Best For**
- Young professionals  
- Couples  
- Lifestyle travelers  
- Content creators  
- Weekend vacationers  

**What to Expect**
- Stylish, fully furnished apartments  
- High-speed Wi-Fi  
- Proximity to cafés, beaches, and lounges  
- Quiet estates for relaxation  

---

#### 2. Victoria Island (VI)  
**Best for Luxury, Business & High-End Experiences**

Victoria Island is Lagos’ financial and lifestyle core, home to corporate offices and luxury entertainment.

**Why Stay in VI**
- Premier central location  
- High security and excellent infrastructure  
- Fine dining, lounges, and event centers  
- Close to beaches  

**Best For**
- Corporate travelers  
- Luxury seekers  
- First-time Lagos visitors  
- Event attendees  

**What to Expect**
- Luxury serviced apartments  
- Cityline and waterfront views  
- Premium furnishings and smart interiors  

---

#### 3. Ikoyi  
**Best for Privacy, Exclusivity & Sophisticated Retreats**

Ikoyi is one of Nigeria’s most prestigious residential districts, offering calm, security, and elegance.

**Why Stay in Ikoyi**
- Extremely secure environment  
- Quiet and refined lifestyle  
- Premium dining and private clubs  
- Green spaces and waterfront views  

**Best For**
- Diplomats  
- Executives  
- Couples seeking privacy  
- Luxury travelers  

**What to Expect**
- Spacious luxury apartments  
- High-end furnishings  
- 24/7 power and private security  
- Peaceful atmosphere  

---

#### 4. Ikeja GRA  
**Best for Business Travelers & Airport Access**

Ikeja GRA is Lagos Mainland’s most organized and secure area, located close to the airport.

**Why Stay in Ikeja GRA**
- Proximity to Murtala Muhammed Airport  
- Quiet and structured environment  
- Restaurants, lounges, and malls nearby  

**Best For**
- Business travelers  
- Airport transit guests  
- Families  
- Mainland-focused visitors  

**What to Expect**
- Spacious serviced apartments  
- Easy transport routes  
- Access to Ikeja City Mall and leisure spots  

---

#### 5. Yaba  
**Best for Budget-Friendly & Tech-Inspired Weekends**

Yaba is Lagos’ tech and innovation hub, youthful, energetic, and affordable.

**Why Stay in Yaba**
- Budget-friendly shortlets  
- Close to tech hubs and universities  
- Easy access to Island and Mainland  

**Best For**
- Students  
- Tech professionals  
- Budget travelers  
- Group trips  

**What to Expect**
- Clean studio apartments  
- Modern 1-bedroom units  
- Affordable, secure accommodation  

---

#### 6. Ajah  
**Best for Quiet, Relaxing & Affordable Beachside Stays**

Ajah offers a calmer lifestyle compared to central Lekki districts.

**Why Stay in Ajah**
- Less congestion  
- Affordable accommodation  
- Growing social scene  
- Proximity to Lekki Phase 2  

**Best For**
- Couples  
- Families  
- Long-stay guests  
- Travelers seeking peace  

**What to Expect**
- Spacious apartments  
- Secure estates  
- Modern furnishings at lower cost  

---

#### 7. Surulere  
**Best for Lagos Culture & Central Access**

Surulere is rich in history and culture, offering easy access to both the Island and Mainland.

**Why Stay in Surulere**
- Central location  
- Affordable shortlets  
- Lively food and entertainment scene  

**Best For**
- Solo travelers
