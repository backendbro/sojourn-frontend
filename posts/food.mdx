# Street Food Delights in Lagos

## The Flavors of the Streets

Lagos is a haven for food lovers. Street vendors offer an array of dishes that are both spicy and aromatic.

## Culinary Traditions

Many recipes are passed down through generations, preserving authentic flavors and local techniques.

## Must-Try Dishes

From suya and akara to puff-puff and chin-chin, every bite is a taste of West African heritage.
